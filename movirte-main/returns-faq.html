How to Return

To initiate a return, email us at returns@movirte.com with your order number and reason for return. We will send you a prepaid returns label within 24 hours (UK orders) or return instructions (international orders).

Please ensure items are unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached before sending them back.

You have 14 days from the date of delivery to return items. Items must be in their original, unworn condition with all tags still attached. We recommend trying items on carefully to ensure they remain in returnable condition.

All returned items must be:

  • Unworn and unwashed
  • In original condition with no signs of wear, damage, or alterations
  • With all original tags attached
  • In original packaging where possible

Items that do not meet these conditions may be returned to you and a refund will not be issued.

UK customers receive a free prepaid returns label. For international returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. We strongly recommend using a tracked shipping service for international returns, as we cannot be held responsible for items lost in transit.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, your refund will be processed within 5–7 working days to the original payment method. You will receive an email confirmation when your refund has been issued.

Please note that it may take an additional 3–5 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.

Yes, you will receive a full refund for the item(s) returned, provided they meet our return conditions. Original shipping costs are non-refundable. If you received free shipping on your order, the full item price will be refunded.

Sale items and items purchased with a promotional discount are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged. Gift cards are also non-refundable. Please check the product page carefully before purchasing sale items.

If more than 10 working days have passed since you received your refund confirmation email and the funds have not appeared in your account, please first check with your bank or payment provider. If the issue persists, contact us at support@movirte.com and we will investigate.

Exchanges

Yes, we offer free exchanges for UK orders. Email us at returns@movirte.com with your order number and the new size or colour you'd like. Exchanges are subject to stock availability — if your preferred option is unavailable, we will issue a full refund instead.

Once we receive your returned item, we will dispatch the replacement within 2–3 working days. UK delivery of the replacement typically takes 3–5 working days. You will receive tracking information via email.

Due to the complexity of international shipping, we recommend returning the item for a refund and placing a new order for the desired size or colour. Return shipping for international orders is the customer's responsibility. Contact us at returns@movirte.com for assistance.

Damaged or Faulty Items

We're sorry to hear that. Please email support@movirte.com within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and photographs of the damage or fault. We will arrange a free return and send you a replacement or issue a full refund, whichever you prefer.

If you received an incorrect item, please contact us immediately at support@movirte.com with a photo of what you received and your order number. We will arrange collection of the incorrect item at no cost and dispatch the correct item right away.

Other

Yes, gifts can be returned within the 14-day return window. The refund will be issued to the original purchaser's payment method, or we can issue a store credit to the gift recipient. Please email returns@movirte.com with the order number to arrange.

Yes, we can issue store credit as an alternative to a refund upon request. Store credit never expires and can be used on any future purchase. Contact us at returns@movirte.com to request this option.

Our customer support team is available Monday to Friday, 9am–6pm GMT. You can reach us at support@movirte.com or use the live chat on our website. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours.
