Returns & Exchanges

We offer free returns and exchanges on all UK orders within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

Sale items and gift cards are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

To initiate a return, email us at returns@movirte.com with your order number and reason for return. We will send you a prepaid returns label within 24 hours.

Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 5–7 working days to the original payment method.

Yes, we offer free exchanges for UK orders. Simply email us at returns@movirte.com with your order number and the size you'd like to exchange to. Subject to availability.

International returns are accepted but the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a tracked service. Once received, your refund will be processed within 5–7 working days.

Product Care

To preserve the quality and longevity of your garments, we recommend:

  • Machine wash cold (30°C) on a gentle cycle
  • Turn garments inside out before washing
  • Do not tumble dry — hang or lay flat to dry
  • Iron on low heat, inside out
  • Do not bleach or dry clean

Specific care instructions are also included on the label inside each garment.

We are committed to responsible production. Our collections use high-quality fabrics designed to last, reducing the need for frequent replacements. We work with ethical manufacturers and are continuously improving our sustainability practices across the supply chain.
