PRIVACY POLICY

Information we collect

Information you provide to us

When you use our website, create an account, place an order, sign up to our newsletter, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and billing and delivery addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment providers)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Communications you send to us (e.g. customer service enquiries)

Information we collect automatically

When you visit our site, we may automatically collect certain technical data, including your IP address, browser type, device information, and how you use our website (e.g. pages viewed, time spent). We do this via cookies and similar technologies as described below.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders and manage your account
  • Send you order confirmations, shipping updates, and customer service replies
  • Send you our newsletter and marketing communications (where you have agreed)
  • Improve our website, products, and services
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

Legal basis for processing: We process your data where necessary to perform our contract with you (e.g. fulfilling orders), where you have given consent (e.g. marketing), or where we have a legitimate interest (e.g. improving our site), and where required by law.

Cookies

Cookies and other tracking technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and to show you relevant content. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings. For more detail, see our Cookie Policy (if applicable) or contact us.

Who we share your information with

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us run our business (e.g. payment processors, delivery carriers, email and hosting providers)
  • Legal and regulatory bodies when required by law

We do not sell your personal data. We require third parties to use your data only for the purposes we specify and to protect it in line with applicable data protection law.

Third-party websites and links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (e.g. social media, payment or delivery partners). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Retention

We keep your information only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy, including to satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. For example, we typically retain order and account data for a period after your last order or account activity, and marketing preferences until you unsubscribe or ask us to stop.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on it
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, please contact us. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be published on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at Contact or support@movirte.com.
